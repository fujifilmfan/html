What To See

Well-maintained trails provide access to a few of the more than 2,000 natural sandstone arches found in the park. Marvel at their beauty and the contrast between shear mass and delicate balancing acts.

What?

Arches National Park contains diverse sandstone formations, including pinnacles, arches, and fins.

Where?

Arches NP is in Utah, USA.

Price?

$10 for a private vehicle; hikes are free
